## The short version

You are trusting us during one of the hardest seasons of your family's life. So: we collect only what the app needs to work, your private entries stay private, we never sell your data or use it for advertising, and you can delete your account — and everything in it — from inside the app at any time.

## What we collect

- **Account basics:** name, email, language preference, time zone, and whether your account is connected to a provider organization.
- **What you put in the app:** daily check-ins, tracker entries, boundaries ("walls"), letters, messages with coaches, wins you submit, rehearsal counts. Rehearsal **recordings stay on your device** and are never uploaded.
- **Subscription status:** processed by Apple/Google and RevenueCat (our subscription processor). We never see or store full payment card numbers.
- **Technical basics:** device type, app version, crash logs, and de-identified usage events (which screens are used). Sensitive content — letters, check-in notes, messages — is **excluded from analytics**.

## What we never do

No selling or renting personal data. No advertising, ad SDKs, or data brokers. No using your letters, messages, or check-ins to train AI models. No sharing with a provider organization unless your account is attached to it (and then only per the table above) or you explicitly consent to a referral.

## Crisis situations

If we reasonably believe there is imminent danger to your life or someone else's, we may share the minimum necessary information with emergency services (911/988). See our Crisis Support terms in the Terms of Service.

## Legal requests

We disclose data only when legally compelled (valid subpoena/court order), we notify you unless legally barred, and we challenge overbroad requests. *(Counsel: 42 CFR Part 2 does not apply to us as a non-treatment entity — confirm — but courts and opposing parties in custody disputes may target this data; consider a published law-enforcement-request policy.)*

## Retention & deletion

Active accounts: content retained while the account exists. Deletion (Settings → Delete account): personal content hard-deleted within 30 days; backups age out within 90. Crisis call records are retained in de-identified form for safety review. Provider orgs do not retain copies of your private content after deletion.

## Children

Sober Helpline is for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
