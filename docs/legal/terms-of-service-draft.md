## 1. What Sober Helpline is — and is not

Sober Helpline provides **family support, education, and coaching** for families affected by a loved one's substance use: daily check-ins, boundary tools, educational content, peer-support groups, coach messaging and calls, and crisis support routing.

Sober Helpline is **not**: medical care, mental-health treatment, therapy, a substance-use treatment program, or an emergency service. Coaches are not acting as licensed clinicians within the app even when they hold licenses (staff labeled "Counselor" hold verified clinical credentials, but in-app services remain coaching, not treatment). Nothing in the app is a substitute for professional medical or legal advice. **If you or anyone is in immediate danger, call 911. For mental-health crisis, call or text 988.**
